Spaghetti recipe

Description

This homemade Pasta is quite meditarrian with meatballs on top of it. It has a quite unique sauce and the tomatoes are fresh italian ones.

When it comes to the noodles, these are handmade from the michelin star gourmet, Alejandro Sanchez. 100 % Bio.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams meat
  • 100 grams Parmameat
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. form meatballs
  2. prepare the vegetables
  3. tomatoes and sour cream in the pan
  4. cook everything for 40-50 min.
