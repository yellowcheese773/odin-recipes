Gulasch recipe

Description

This is a homemade Gulasch from the hungarian star chef Mischael Houruszkagr.

The meat is from the ribs of the cow, finest muscle, from the mountains of the caucasus.

These cows have better life quality then most of New Yorkers couldnt even dream of. So basically thats why the meat is so good, therefore the Gulasch too.

Ingredients

  • meat
  • tomatos
  • pepper and sault
  • tomato sauce

Steps

  1. prepare the meat
  2. cut the tomatos
  3. make the sauce by only adding salt and pepper
  4. put everything in one pot and cook it
